Pomodoro Studying

How to use

Pomodoro Studying is an application that helps you to organize your studying time well using the Pomodoro way. In the Pomodoro, you study 25 minutes; because the human brain cannot stand more than 25 minutes of studying constantly. Then, 5 minutes of break time to separate your brain from focusing in the studying time are enough. After 3 or 4 times doing the above, you can take a longer break as a reward for your hard studying. You can change the number of minutes by clicking on the minutes number and typing the number you want, you can also change the background or the sound that plays when the time is up. Finally, you can know the current time by the button at the bottom.
Enjoy!

  1. (i) letter = how to use the app
  2. (b) letter = change the background
  3. (s) letter = change the sound
  4. Alt = change the number of minutes
  5. Arrows = change the timer / show explanation and keyboard shortcuts
  6. Space = play/pause timer
  7. Backspace = reset timer
  8. Enter = OK
  9. Tab = switch the mode (Pomodoro Timer, Current Time)
